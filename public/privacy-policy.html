Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Latero ("we", "us", "our") is a distraction-free personal management app. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Latero mobile application and web platform.

2. Information Collection

We collect only the following information to provide our services:

  • Email address (for account creation and login)

3. Usage of Information

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Authenticated access to your saved items

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database management. Your data is processed securely through their infrastructure. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or other third-party entities.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption and secure database policies (Row Level Security), to protect your personal information.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account and data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.
